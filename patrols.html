1st Trinidad Sea Scouts 2019 - 2020

Patrol Listing

The First Trinidad Sea Scouts troop is split into 4 patrols: Seagulls Tadpoles, Curlews Dolphins, Otters Shrimps, and Beavers Seals. Recruits who are not yet placed into patrols are placed into a temporary patrol called Squids. The troop is managed by the Scout Leader, and is led by the Troop Leader - TL and Assistant Troop Leaders - ATL. The troop leaders are typically in Form 6. Each patrol, which is comprised of student from Form 1 - 5, is led by a Patrol Leader - PL and Assistant Patrol Leader - APL. A Junior patrol is further split from each patrol and is led by the Junior Patrol Leader - JPL. The Junior patrol includes students in Forms 1 - 3.
