Knots

Here are the different categories of knots that scouts should be familiar with.

Basic Knots

Every scout should know these basic knots. These are requierd for the Scout Standard Badge.

  • Reef (Square) Knot
  • Clove Hitch
  • Bowline
  • Round Turn and Two Half Hitches
  • Sheepshank
  • Sheet Bend
  • Figure of Eight Knot

Advanced Knots

The Advanced Knots are more difficult and have more specialized uses. These are required for the Advanced Scout Standard and the Chief Scout Award

  • Fishermans Knot
  • Timber Hitch
  • Triple Bowline
  • Fireman's Chair

Splices

Splices are used to repair frayed rope

  • Back Splice
  • Eye Splice
  • Short Splice

Whippings

Whippings are used to prevent ropes from fraying

  • Simple
  • West Country

Lashings

Lashings are used to bind together two or more spars.

  • Square
  • Diagonal
  • Sheer
  • Tripod
