Patrol Listing

1TSS is divided into four patrols: Seagulls Tadpoles, Curlews Dolphins, Otters Shrimps, and Beavers Seals. Recruits who are not yet placed into patrols are placed into a temporary patrol called Squids. Click below view the patrols or the past scouts directory.
